Membership Application for Non-Profit

Ryan Casey was the software architect and lead developer of Client's custom web application.  This non-profit trade association supports its membership through member events and lobbying efforts.

The web site allows members to join online, pay dues online, register for events and submit annual awards across a variety of categories.

The client's staff is able to fully manage all aspects of the site without ongoing technology support or consulting, excluding feature enhancement requests.

The web site was first developed approximately 11 years ago and the “code base” or core of the application is now used by three other organizations to manage their members.
