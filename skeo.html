Contracts & Project Management Application

Skeo Solutions, an environmental consulting firm, needed a business solution to manage its contracts and projects efficiently.  Ryan Casey was the architect of the Contracts and Project Management (CPM) application, which was designed to meet Skeo's goals of improving efficiency and giving project managers and senior managers the ability to see how resources are expended both at the project level and across the company. 

CPM includes an automated process to create a monthly financial status report that previously took approximately two weeks to generate.  Now the report can be generated in minutes.
